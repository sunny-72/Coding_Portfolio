About Me

I am Sunny Kumar, a dedicated and ambitious student from Champaran, Bihar. I am currently pursuing my Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) in Computer Science and Engineering (C.S.E.) from Guru Jambheshwar University of Science and Technology (GJU), Hisar. I have just completed my third year of studies and am set to enter my final year, equipped with a solid foundation and a passion for software development. ...

My academic journey in Computer Science and Engineering has provided me with comprehensive knowledge and hands-on experience in various programming languages and technologies. I am proficient in C++ and Python, and have gained experience in Java, JavaScript. My technical expertise extends to the MERN stack, which includes MongoDB, Express.js, React.js, and Node.js, showcasing my capability to build full-stack web applications.

My practical experience includes a web developer internship, where I worked on developing an e-commerce website. During this internship, I utilized HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to create a functional and user-friendly online store. One of my notable projects is a web-based news application. This project, built using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Node.js, and Express.js, automatically fetches news from an API and displays it on the website. This project highlights my ability to integrate APIs and create dynamic web applications.
I am also working on a project called StayAway, an application similar to Airbnb, which aims to enhance the travel experience by providing functionalities such as user authentication and property listings. This project demonstrates my interest in real-world applications and my capability to develop complex systems.
